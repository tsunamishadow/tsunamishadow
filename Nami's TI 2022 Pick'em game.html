What do you mean by "a team that finishes below another team" and "top X (regardless of order)"?

I understand this can be confusing so this will explain it. Imagine the table on the left is the result, and on the right is your prediction for a similar prediction game.

For each team in your prediction, they are compared pairwise to each team above them. If the pairwise in your prediction matches the one in the final result, then you score a point.
So for Alliance, you score 5 points, since they are bottom and you placed them lower than each other team.
This is also true for Talon, 4 points for that.
For Entity, you would be correct for them finishing under Secret and Aster, but incorrect for finishing under Thunder Awaken. So 2 points for that.
Similarly for Thunder Awaken, you get 2 points.
Secret also scores a point.

Then for top X, you score a point for each Top X (in this case until Top 5) that is correct.
Top 1 is correct, Top 2 is correct, Top 3 is not, Top 4 is correct, and Top 5 is correct; as such you'll get 4 points.

This is done specifically to benefit most predictions, since swapping a couple of teams - compared to the ending result - would not punish you as hard compared to most pick'em systems, which usually only care about placement.

However if you do pick the group perfectly you will always score the full number of points, while picking the complete reverse order of them finishing scores you nothing.

It sounds more confusing than it actually is, the important thing is to think about where teams will place in comparison to others, rather than just finishing place in the group.

Rules

There is no entry fee.

Entries for LCQ Group Stage and Group Stage Proper will be done by survey. LCQ Playoffs and Main Event entries will be done through Challonge. In order to claim a prize you must be the following:

  • Have a Steam account that has not been Dota 2 Game Banned or have any VAC bans. (You don't have to play Dota 2, you just have to be not banned from it.)
  • Must have a verified PayPal account.
  • Must not be a Dota 2 professional player, talent or Valve employee in the past 2 years.
Anyone is welcome to play otherwise, but you need at least two of (to keep track of your score):
  • A Discord Username
  • A Steam ID
  • A Challonge Account
  • An email address

Dispensations for the above IDs may be given if I know you, none of these IDs will be shared with any entity/person/company. Main Event predictions do not have to match your in game Battle Pass predictions.

Entries are valid right up until the deadline. After deadline times will not be accepted. After the deadline all predictions are made public for everyone to see, but will remain private until the deadline. You can make more than one entry, but only your last entry will be the counted. If you miss a phase you can still enter for later phases, you just wont score for the phases you missed.

I, Nami, will be playing along too.

If you have any other questions, or any suggestions, don't hesitate to contact me on twitter @tsunamishadow or Discord tsunamishadow#2791.
