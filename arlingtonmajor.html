Group A Qualify for Upper Bracket

  • PSG.LGD have already secured first place
  • If OG win 2-0, Royal win 2-0 AND Outsiders win 1-1, there will be a four way tiebreaker for the upper bracket including them and Fnatic. If any of the aformentioned don't happen, Fnatic have Upper Bracket.
  • Outsiders can guarantee Upper bracket with a 2-0 (over Soniqs). A 1-1 will likely put them in Upper Bracket. An 0-2 will force them to Lower Bracket if OG win 2-0 AND Royal win 2-0.
  • OG (over Talon) can guarantee at least an upper bracket playoff with a 2-0.
  • Royal (over Liquid) can guarantee at least an upper bracket playoff with a 2-0
  • Liquid (over Royal) need a 2-0 to have a chance at upper bracket.
  • Talon (over OG) need a 2-0 to have a chance at upper bracket, while depending on other results.
  • Thunder (over LGD) need a 2-0 to have a chance at upper bracket, while depending on other results.
  • Soniqs cannot get upper bracket, but are not yet eliminated.
